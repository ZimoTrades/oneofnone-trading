Purpose

What this community is built around

This server exists for one purpose — to build traders who understand how to research, validate, and automate a strategy properly.

The community is built around the belief that retail trading requires a systematic, evidence-based approach to work long-term. That means hypothesis-driven research, automated validation, and full strategy automation. Everything here is oriented around that process.

The focus is on process, methodology, and execution. Signals, tips, and predictions are outside the scope of this community.
